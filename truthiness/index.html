Truthiness — Privacy Policy

Publisher: Trimtab Labs · Effective date: 25 June 2026
Applies to the Truthiness — Fact-Check Any Selection Chrome extension.

Truthiness fact-checks text you explicitly select. It is built to collect as little as possible and to forward and forget — the text you check is never stored on our server.

What gets sent, and where

A fact-check runs only when you select text and choose a Truthiness action. The selected text is then sent to an AI model to be checked:

  • Free Quick check (default): the selection is sent through our proxy to Google Gemini and the result is returned to you. The proxy does not store, log, or retain the text — only anonymous aggregate counts (for rate-limiting and totals). On this free tier, the AI provider (Google) may use submitted content to improve its models under its own terms.
  • Verify (optional paid tier): the selection is sent through our proxy to xAI (Grok) with live web search, and the result — including source links — is returned to you. The proxy does not store the text.
  • Bring-your-own-key (optional): if you enter your own Google, xAI, or OpenAI API key in Options, the selection is sent directly from your browser to that provider using your key and does not pass through our server. Your key is stored locally on your device only.

What we store

On our server: a random install identifier (used only for rate-limiting and abuse prevention) and, if you subscribe, your subscription status tied to that identifier. We do not store your email or the text you check. In your browser: your settings and the install identifier (chrome.storage.local).

Payments

Subscriptions are processed by Stripe. Your email and payment details are handled by Stripe under Stripe's privacy policy; we receive only whether an active subscription exists for your install. We never see or store your card details.

What we do not do

No analytics, no tracking, no advertising, no selling or sharing of data, and no access to your browsing history. The extension reads page content only for the specific selection you fact-check, only when you trigger it.

Permissions

contextMenus (the right-click actions), storage (your settings + install id), activeTab + scripting (to draw the results panel on the page when you click), and host access limited to our proxy and the supported AI providers (used only to run a check you initiated).

Limited Use

Truthiness's use of information complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
