Zonewise — Privacy Policy

Publisher: Trimtab Labs · Effective date: 24 June 2026
Applies to the Zonewise — Time Zone Converter & World Clock Chrome extension.

Zonewise does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

What we store

The only thing Zonewise saves is the list of cities you choose to display. It is stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage.local) and never leaves your device.

What we do not do

Zonewise makes no network requests, has no backend server, and contains no analytics, tracking, advertising, or accounts. It cannot access your browsing history, the contents of web pages, or any other website. All time-zone conversion runs on your device using the browser's built-in internationalization engine.

Permissions

Zonewise requests a single permission, storage, used only to remember your chosen cities between sessions.

Data sharing

None — we have no data to share because we collect none.

Limited Use

Zonewise's use of information complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
