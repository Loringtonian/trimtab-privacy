You're all set — Speed Reader

Thanks for installing! Speed Reader is a free speed reader that flashes your text one word at a time so your eyes stop wandering and you read faster — often twice as fast within a few sessions. Paste anything, or right-click any text on any page, and it plays the words to you at a speed you control.

Start reading faster in 10 seconds

  1. Give it some text

    Click the toolbar icon and paste text into the popup — or highlight text on any page, right-click, and choose “Speed-read selection.”

  2. Press Play

    The reader goes fullscreen and flashes one word at a time, with the focus letter highlighted so your eye locks onto the right spot. Press Esc to exit.

  3. Push the speed up

    Slide the WPM (words per minute) control higher as your brain adapts. Start comfortable, then climb — most readers comfortably double their reading speed.

Good to know

  • Built-in WPM test — read a passage and watch your reading speed climb.
  • One word or chunks — a single word for maximum speed, or two or three together for context.
  • Your colours — System, Match page, Light, Dark, or fully custom; pick your font and text size.
  • Completely free — no account, no sign-up, no subscription.
  • Private by design — everything runs on your device. No network requests, no tracking, no ads. Speed Reader never stores or sends the text you read.
